About Us

The AQK Foundation was established to continue the legacy of Abdul Qayyum Khan (RA), dedicated to supporting the disadvantaged and promoting sustainable community development.

Our Mission

Relieving poverty, advancing education, promoting health, and supporting sustainable projects for communities in need.

Our Activities

Monthly food distributions, emergency aid grants, orphan sponsorship, digital literacy programs, healthcare clinics, clean water projects, and vocational training.
