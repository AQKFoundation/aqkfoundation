Our Mission

In loving memory of Abdul Qayyum Khan (RA), the AQK Foundation was established to continue his lifelong dedication to serving the underprivileged in the Moulvibazar district of Bangladesh. We aim to relieve poverty, advance education, promote health, and foster sustainable development, all while fulfilling our Zakat obligations according to Islamic teachings.

First-Year Activity Plan

  • Poverty Relief: Monthly food distributions and emergency aid to widows and orphans (Zakat-compliant).
  • Educational Empowerment: Sponsoring 20 orphaned children's education and establishing a basic computer lab.
  • Health and Hygiene: Organising free health clinics and distributing hygiene kits.
  • Community Development: Installing tube wells and delivering vocational training for young adults.
